# Limitations

## Scientific Scope

- Scope is limited to Stage 1 FFF total orders and cyclic groups.
- The included default grid is `n,m in {1,2,3,4}`.
- The finite grid is a declared computational grid, not a biological sampling distribution.
- The capsule does not include permutation groups, measurable spaces, FBT appendix arithmetic, evolutionary dynamics, ML/RL, or structure-transfer metrics.
- The package contains no scientific figures and no browser demo.

## Interpretation Limits

- The exact counts support only the listed `CLM-...` rows.
- The `RDR-0002` unique-function columns are presentation companions and do not replace the source orientation-witness count.
- No result in this capsule establishes a claim about real organisms, human perception, metaphysics, spacetime, or consciousness.
