# TASK-004-P4-CAPSULE: Stage 4 FBT Atlas Checkpoint Capsule

TASK ID: TASK-004-P4-CAPSULE
EPISTEMIC STATUS: E

## Research Question

Can the reviewed Stage 4 FBT finite-atlas spec/oracle/grid-smoke bundle be packaged
into a repository-local checkpoint capsule without expanding scientific claims or
starting a full atlas run?

## Primary Source

- `SRC-FBT-2021`
- Version of record: https://doi.org/10.1007/s10441-020-09400-0
- Author manuscript used for open source access:
  https://sites.socsci.uci.edu/~ddhoff/FitnessBeatsTruth_apa_PBR

## Formal Definitions

This task packages definitions and implementation already completed by:

- `TASK-004-FBT-ATLAS-SPEC`
- `TASK-004-FBT-ATLAS-ORACLE`
- `TASK-004-FBT-ATLAS-GRID-V0`

Canonical Stage 4 specs:

- `specs/fbt/theorem4_domain.md`
- `specs/fbt/finite_atlas_design.md`

## Input Domain

The capsule input domain is limited to the reviewed Stage 4 finite-cell bundle:

- committed source/spec fixture cases in `tests/fixtures/fbt/stage4_fbt_spec_cases.json`;
- committed oracle fixture cases in `tests/fixtures/fbt/stage4_fbt_oracle_cases.json`;
- frozen smoke-grid config in `experiments/configs/fbt_atlas_v0.json`;
- deterministic `fbt_atlas_v0` smoke output generated by
  `fts fbt atlas-grid-v0-smoke`.

No full atlas, stochastic simulation, evolutionary dynamics, theorem-probability
calculation, ML/RL, dashboard, notebook, or figure is introduced.

## Invariants

- The capsule must not change Stage 4 production oracle or grid semantics.
- `CLM-FBT-ATLAS-001` may be packaged only as a reviewed spec/oracle/grid-smoke bundle.
- `CLM-FBT-THM-001` remains not theorem-implemented.
- Grid-smoke aggregates remain `grid_frequency` values for `fbt_atlas_v0` only.
- The capsule checksum inventory must list every committed capsule file except
  `checksums.txt`.

## Assumptions

No new scientific assumption is introduced.

Approved Stage 4 assumptions used by the reviewed bundle:

- `ASM-FBT-0001`: MAP ties are represented as full MAP sets.
- `ASM-FBT-0002`: zero-marginal observations remain undefined.
- `ASM-FBT-0003`: finite grids report `grid_frequency`, not theorem probability.
- `ASM-FBT-0004`: primary comparison is `truth_map` versus
  `fitness_only_expected`.

## Claims Allowed

- The reviewed Stage 4 FBT finite-atlas spec/oracle/grid-smoke bundle has been
  packaged as a repository-local checkpoint capsule.
- The capsule links `CLM-FBT-ATLAS-001` to source, task, assumption, review, manifest,
  and checksum records for the limited reviewed scope.
- `REV-TASK-004-FBT-ATLAS-001` accepted that bundle with no fatal, major, or minor
  findings.

## Claims Forbidden

- Do not claim the capsule is a GitHub release, DOI archive, external publication, or
  final public Stage 4 paper.
- Do not claim a full atlas run.
- Do not claim Theorem 4 has been implemented, proved, reproduced, or reviewed as a
  theorem implementation.
- Do not treat `fbt_atlas_v0` grid frequencies as source theorem probabilities.
- Do not claim anything about real perception, consciousness, spacetime, ontology,
  biology, ML/RL, or evolutionary dynamics.

## Out Of Scope

- new production engine work;
- atlas v1 design;
- full finite atlas enumeration;
- stochastic simulation;
- figures, UI, dashboards, or notebooks;
- GitHub release, tag, DOI, or archival deposit;
- independent review in this same implementation session.
